Gold and brass pieces

Gold jewelry has a rich, warm tone that can complement a wide range of color palettes. When styling or designing around gold jewelry, it’s essential to choose colors that either enhance the warmth of gold or create a striking contrast.

Silver pieces

Silver jewelry, with its cool, sleek tones, pairs well with a variety of color palettes that either enhance its icy elegance or create a striking contrast. Silver is incredibly versatile and works across different styles, whether modern, casual, or glamorous.

Color Analysis

Color analysis is a powerful tool for aligning your wardrobe, makeup, and even hair color with your natural features. By understanding whether your coloring is warm or cool, light or dark, muted or bright, you can choose colors that make you look and feel your best.
